Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding your personal information while using Getzio Focus.

1. Overview

Getzio Focus is a modern productivity and task management app designed to help you organize your daily work efficiently. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through compliance with this policy. This application does not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties.

🔑

Information You Provide

Authentication Data: When registering or logging into Getzio Focus, you provide your phone number. This is processed securely via Firebase Authentication to authenticate you and send one-time OTP (One-Time Password) codes.

App Data & Tasks: Any tasks, subtasks, checklists, and configurations you create within the app are processed and stored locally on your device using an encrypted local database (Hive) to ensure fast, offline-first operation.

⚡

Automatically Collected Info

Device Information: We may collect technical details such as your operating system version (iOS/Android), device model, and unique identifiers solely to resolve application crashes, diagnostic errors, and performance optimizations.

Usage Statistics: Standard performance telemetry may be logged anonymously to diagnose connectivity and auth latency issues.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information only for the following explicit purposes:

  • To provide and maintain services: Creating and managing your secure account.
  • Authentication & Security: Sending verification codes and preventing fraud.
  • Offline Caching: Keeping your tasks accessible offline through your device storage.
  • Diagnostics: Resolving application crashes, error codes, and optimizing UI performance.

3. Third-Party Services

To enable core app features, we integrate with trusted third-party providers who adhere to strict privacy standards:

🔥
Firebase Authentication Provides secure phone number registry and OTP verification.

No other analytics trackers, advertising networks, or third-party marketing services are embedded in Getzio Focus.

4. Account & Data Deletion

Important for App Store Review

Under Apple App Store requirements, you have the absolute right to delete your account and all associated personal data from our systems at any time.

How to request data deletion:

  1. Send an email to our support team at shihadpalakkad@gmail.com.
  2. Include the phone number used for registration in your email.
  3. Your account registration record, authentication profile, and configuration details will be permanently wiped from Firebase and our servers within 48 hours of verification.

5. Security of Your Data

The security of your personal information is extremely important to us. Your locally stored task database is sandboxed by your mobile operating system. Authentication requests are encrypted via SSL/TLS during transmission to Firebase. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.
